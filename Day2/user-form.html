About Hogwarts

Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, established centuries ago, is an esteemed magical institution located in Scotland. Within its ancient castle, students embark on a transformative seven-year journey of magical education. Sorting into one of four houses Gryffindor, Hufflepuff, Ravenclaw, or Slytherin they learn spells, potions, and defend against the dark arts.
